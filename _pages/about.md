At the moment, I'm working as a UROP in the Camera Culture group at the Media Lab working on decentralized AI. My research interests span the fields of **reinforcement learning** and **natural language processing**.

Recently, I worked on a new prompt engineering technique inspired by Daniel Kahneman's **dual-process theory of the mind**, which was created with an interdisciplinary approach for smarter models that I hope to pursue as a research direction to help create systems that are both scientifically robust and aligned with human cognitive processes.

I was born and raised in East Los Angeles, California, where I spent a lot of my time reading and tinkering with as many electronics as I could get my hands on. That tinkering grew into a passion for creating intelligent systems, and that same passion motivates me in my work today.

I hope to use my passion for NLP and RL along with plenty of my time to push the boundary forward in the field of machine learning and move one step closer to the goal of truly intelligent machines.
