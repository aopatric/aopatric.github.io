### Goals for the New Year

I think some of my biggest goals are personal this year, but mostly mental/physical health related.

My biggest academic goal is to have work accepted at a conference in the year 2025, and get one step closer to my goal of grad school in machine learning.

I'll be taking analysis next semester, which is honestly pretty exciting and is partially motivating my reading of Velleman's *How to Prove It*. I hope to continue doing research in AI and hopefully narrow in on my goals of RL and NLP research this year.

#### Also: predictions...

I sincerely believe AGI (if not ASI) will come about soon. The current research is leaning further and further toward RL for NLP, and when we taught RL how to play Go, it beat the best human. When we taught RL how to move in simulated environments, it become Spot. When we teach RL how to reason, what will happen? I think we'll find out sometime this year.
